Our Story

Tradition. Passion. Family.

Our Heritage

Bringing the Island's Flavors to You

Ceylon Spice was born from a simple desire: to share the rich, diverse, and incredible flavors of Sri Lankan cuisine with the world. Founded by a family with roots in the southern coastal town of Galle, we grew up surrounded by the aroma of roasted curry powder, the sound of sizzling hoppers, and the warmth of family gatherings.

Our recipes have been passed down through generations. We believe in authenticity above all else. That means grinding our own spices, squeezing fresh coconut milk daily, and cooking with the same patience and love that our grandmothers did.

Whether you are looking for the fiery kick of a black pork curry or the comforting embrace of a coconut sambol, we invite you to join us at our table. Come as a guest, leave as family.
